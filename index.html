About STRIDE

A student technical team which focuses on developing a quadruped which can walk autonomously on all terrain. We are a team of undergraduate students spanning various branches of engineering.

Our Team was Started in August 2019, by some undergraduate students working in Tinkerers' Lab, aiming to develop a completely autonomous Quadruped Bot which can be used in applications such as disaster management, exploring unknown territory and doing search operation in an uneven terrain

Our Team has following Subsections:

  • Robot Design and Modelling
  • Robotic Controls
  • Localisation
  • Robot Fabrication and Assembly
